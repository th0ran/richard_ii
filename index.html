At 12.55 David Tennant and director Gregory Doran will be live in the studio to answer questions submitted by schools across the country. Questions can be submitted between 8.30 – 9.30 and during the scheduled breaks. Questions should not be submitted during the broadcast itself.

Only one question of 140 character limit can submitted at a time. When you are ready to submit a question fill out the form on the film page and click to submit. To give your school’s questions the best chance of being selected, try to ensure that they are about the performances, the production, the directors choices, or the play itself. It may help to ask students to write their suggestions down on paper as they watch the production, before selecting the best ones to be submitted online.

Information For Your IT Team

To access the broadcast we recommend the latest version of your browser (Internet Explorer, Chrome etc.) and the latest version of Adobe Flash. Please contact your ISP System Administrator about possible firewall restrictions, filters or blocks on streaming media. The test stream will be an important opportunity for you to identify any technical issues in advance of the event.

Broadcast Recomendations

We recommend that the broadcast is viewed on one large screen per classroom of up to 30 students, with a maximum of 10 computers accessing the broadcast per school in total. Please bear in mind that additional screens will use up more of your school's bandwidth and may result in buffering.

Compatibility With Mobile Devices

Please note that the classroom stream is not intended to be viewed on mobile devices such as iPads, iPhones and Android devices. For this reason they are unsupported to access the broadcast

Live Stream Only

Please note that the broadcast is only available to watch live. It will not be available to download and cannot be accessed at a later date. If you are not able to watch the live broadcast with your students, contact your local cinema to find out if they are offering schools encore screenings.

Logging in

To access the stream, teaching staff should be logged in to their workstation or laptop, preferably as either a 'power user' or as one with local administrator privileges. In addition, preferred internet browsers are Google Chrome or Mozilla Firefox. The workstation or laptop should be connected to a broadband internet connection and have a fully up-to-date version of Adobe Flash Player installed. Please contact your ISP System Administrator about possible firewall restrictions, filters or blocks on streaming media

If you have any trouble logging on please email education@rsc.org.uk or call 01789 403432
